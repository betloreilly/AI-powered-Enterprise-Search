# LEXORA Support Knowledge Base

## Orders & Shipping

### How do I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

### What are your shipping options?
We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), express shipping (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping (next business day). Shipping costs vary by option and location.

### Can I change or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, contact our support team immediately for assistance.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and with tags attached. Some items like electronics may have different return windows.

### How do I return an item?
Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the item you want to return, and follow the return process. You'll receive a prepaid return label via email.

### When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will appear in your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or credit card company.

## FlexCard

### What is FlexCard?
FlexCard is LEXORA's digital gift card and store credit system. It can be used for purchases across most product categories.

### How do I check my FlexCard balance?
You can check your FlexCard balance in your account dashboard or at checkout. The balance is displayed in real-time.

### Does my FlexCard expire?
Yes, FlexCards expire after 12 months from issuance. Promotional FlexCards may have shorter expiration periods. Check your account dashboard for specific expiration dates.

### Can I use FlexCard with other payment methods?
Yes, FlexCard can be combined with credit cards, debit cards, or other accepted payment methods for partial payments.

## Care+ Protection Plans

### What is Care+?
Care+ is an extended warranty and protection plan that covers your products beyond the manufacturer warranty, including accidental damage protection.

### Care+ Basic Plan
- **Eligibility**: All products
- **Coverage**: 1 year extended warranty
- **Protection**: Mechanical and electrical failures
- **Cost**: Varies by product category

### Care+ Premium Plan
- **Eligibility**: Electronics and high-value items
- **Coverage**: 2 years extended warranty
- **Protection**: Mechanical failures, electrical failures, accidental damage (drops, spills)
- **Priority Support**: Dedicated support line
- **Cost**: Higher than Basic, varies by product

### How do I file a Care+ claim?
Contact LEXORA support and provide proof of purchase and issue description. Claim processing typically takes 3-5 business days.

### What does Care+ cover?
Care+ includes extended warranty coverage, accidental damage protection (drops, spills), mechanical and electrical failure coverage, and priority customer support.

### Care+ Plan Comparison

| Plan | Eligibility | Coverage Period | Accidental Damage | Priority Support | Cost |
|------|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|------------------|------|
| Care+ Basic | All products | 1 year | No | No | Lower |
| Care+ Premium | Electronics only | 2 years | Yes | Yes | Higher |

## SmartReturn

### What is SmartReturn?
SmartReturn is LEXORA's hassle-free return program that allows eligible customers to return items within 30 days with free return shipping.

### Which products are eligible for SmartReturn?
Most new products are eligible. Some items like electronics, personalized products, or final sale items may have restrictions. Check product details for eligibility.

### How do I use SmartReturn?
During checkout, eligible items will show the SmartReturn badge. Simply initiate a return through your account, and we'll provide a prepaid return label.

## Account & Payment

### How do I update my account information?
Log into your account, go to "Account Settings", and update your personal information, shipping addresses, or payment methods.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and FlexCard.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

## Product Information

### How can I return a product?
To return a product, log into your account, navigate to "My Orders", select the item you want to return, and follow the return process. You'll receive a prepaid return label via email if you're eligible for SmartReturn.

### What is your warranty policy?
All products come with manufacturer warranties. You can extend coverage with Care+ protection plans. Check individual product pages for specific warranty details.
